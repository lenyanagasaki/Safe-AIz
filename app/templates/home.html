A Yolov5 model to detect weapons in a image, video, or live feed

How Our Product Works

We trained yolov5 on our custom dataset of guns, blades, airsoft, and toys to create a model that can identify and discern what the weapon is.

Gathering images

We used SerpAPI to webscrape for images to use for our dataset

Labeling

We used Roboflow to label all of our data into our selective classes:

  • Airsoft Gun (Orange Tip)
  • Toy Gun
  • Gun
  • Blade

    • Training

    Once we had our labelled data, we trained our model using Google Colab and Kaggle.
